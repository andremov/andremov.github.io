Genetic Battle Bots

Personal project. Meant to help the understanding of genetic algorithms, as a simple usage of bots built based on a genetic code that battle each other.

Gallery

The purpose of this project was to learn and understand the functionality, and purpose of genetic algorithms.

Based on a few videos on YouTube about genetic algorithms, a little on how they work, and the simulations the algorithm ran.

Allows multiple breeding options.

Allows varying parameters.

Due to the way the algorithm and application work, multiple "correct" answers are possible, which is a little against the idea of genetic algorithms. Further research may be required, another genetic algorithm project will be made in the future.

Worked on January 2018. (1 week).
